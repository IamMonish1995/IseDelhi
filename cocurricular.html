    • Introdunction

    All round development of students makes their personality outstanding! We understand that education cannot be just one dimensional. It has to be approached holistically to inculcate life skills in students so that they can excel integrally. The co-curricular program and activities at Maxfort are a step in this positive direction, breaking away from the pressures of academics and enriching student's talents and skills in areas that interest them. The school runs a large plethora of activities to cater to the individual needs of students so that they can pursue and master the skills they love, in a supportive and congenial environment. The co-curricular activities not only help the students taste success and unravel their own personal worth but also develop a sense of community and team work. Besides, the students learn self discipline, commitment and gain self confidence. The broad spectrum of co-curricular activities provided by Maxfort is based on the core belief that nurturing talent and recognizing abilities of students helps to straighten their personalities, promote collaboration among them and preserves their passion for learning and knowledge. Moreover, the program gives the students an opportunity to have fun and discover their inner joy.

    Sports

    An exciting and fun sport, badminton requires only a small feathered shuttle and racquet to indulge in an all-round activity that boosts both mental and physical health. A simple yet interesting game, Badminton has tremendous energy factor associated with it.
    Maxfort prides itself in providing well laid out badminton courts for students to play and learn.
    Our trained and highly proficient coaches help the students to learn and enjoy the game as they run, dive and lunge to hit the shuttle. The fast pace of the game and the mobility that comes with it, keep the students on their toes and extremely fit. Besides, the sport helps to develop deep bonding and friendship during and after the game, thus improving the social skills of children.
    At Maxfort, we encourage our students to participate in this leisure as well as competitive sport and experience its numerous benefits.
