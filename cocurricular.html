Introduction

All round development of students makes their personality outstanding! We understand that education cannot be just one dimensional. It has to be approached holistically to inculcate life skills in students so that they can excel integrally. The co-curricular program and activities at ISE Delhi are a step in this positive direction, breaking away from the pressures of academics and enriching student's talents and skills in areas that interest them. The school runs a large plethora of activities to cater to the individual needs of students so that they can pursue and master the skills they love, in a supportive and congenial environment. The co-curricular activities not only help the students taste success and unravel their own personal worth but also develop a sense of community and team work. Besides, the students learn self discipline, commitment and gain self confidence. The broad spectrum of co-curricular activities provided by ISE Delhi is based on the core belief that nurturing talent and recognizing abilities of students helps to straighten their personalities, promote collaboration among them and preserves their passion for learning and knowledge. Moreover, the program gives the students an opportunity to have fun and discover their inner joy.

Sports

An exciting and fun sport, badminton requires only a small feathered shuttle and racquet to indulge in an all-round activity that boosts both mental and physical health. A simple yet interesting game, Badminton has tremendous energy factor associated with it.
ISE Delhi prides itself in providing well laid out badminton courts for students to play and learn.
Our trained and highly proficient coaches help the students to learn and enjoy the game as they run, dive and lunge to hit the shuttle. The fast pace of the game and the mobility that comes with it, keep the students on their toes and extremely fit. Besides, the sport helps to develop deep bonding and friendship during and after the game, thus improving the social skills of children.
At ISE Delhi, we encourage our students to participate in this leisure as well as competitive sport and experience its numerous benefits.

Music

Music is the soul of the universe. It gives wings to the mind, flight to imagination and mellow to the heart. Music puts life into everything.
What words cannot express deeply, music expresses it effortlessly. It speaks a language that can be understood by everyone. It heals the heart and calms the soul.
Music is therapeutic as it increases optimism, hope and competence. It is proven by scientific research that when children during their growth involved in music always show better academic performance, lower rates of addictive behaviour and more proficiency at work.
At ISE Delhi, we provide our students ample opportunities to learn and practice both classical and popular music. Similarly, we have proper learning facilities for our pupil in the domains of vocal and instrumental music.
Always alive and brimming with activities, the music rehearsal rooms in the school are designed to suit the needs of all age groups of students.
Creating an exciting as well as stimulating environment, we aim to provide bright prospects to students to fully explore and become skilled at traditional and contemporary music.
Our teaching faculty is strong in musical skills covering a broad spectrum of instruments and musical acumen. Teaching the students, all aspects of music, our faculty groom them to perfection. One of the liveliest departments-music at ISE Delhi, connects students to their own rhythm.

Theater & Drama

Great theatre is about challenging how we think and encouraging us to fantasize about a world we aspire to create.
Theatre is an opening of a new door before children. It is a world full of vibrancy and excitement. It gives them education and values that are usually absent in the regular curriculum and classroom. In fact, it helps them to go deep within, know themselves and work on their hidden potential.
At ISE Delhi, we understand the deep impact of Theatre and Drama on the life of individuals, particularly students. Studies have shown that students who participate in these activities score well in exams, have better communication skills and presence in school and form stronger bonds with other students.
They also help in developing the interpersonal skills like compassion and empathy which otherwise are not usually gained by any other vocations including sports.
ISE Delhi provides an outstanding multitude of facilities for students to learn, participate and perform in theatre. With a highly qualified professional team that teaches all the nuances of this performing art to students; we foster effective education with the help of theatre techniques.
The Theatre Club at the school promotes genuine artistic talent and with the help of the faculty, the students participate and perform in various activities throughout the year.

Art & Crafts

The desire to create is one of the deepest yearnings of the human soul.
An artist is hidden in every child, irrespective of his talent. Art is as natural as sunshine and as vital as nourishment.
When young children engage in activities of art and craft, it gives them a great experience with lasting memories. Best part is that it also improves their cognition. Accelerating the development of muscles and improving motor skills, art and craft activities give the students a broader perspective about various things. Promoting social bonding, these activities also help to develop language skills.
With an aim to mould young minds and nurturing their creativity, ISE Delhi offers new avenues to pursue art and craft activities in school. Providing creative freedom, the students are encouraged to learn and share through art and artistic endeavor. Pushing students to fantasize, develop aesthetic expression and learn various skills, the diverse art and craft activities at ISE Delhi imbue a sense of responsibility in students as well give wings to their imagination.
