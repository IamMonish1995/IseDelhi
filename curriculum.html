Empowering Curriculum

"In the hustle and bustle of life, never forget that you are essentially divine!
Tat Twam Asi! Thou are that!"

Ise Delhi School's teaching approach with a progressive curriculum

It is rightly said that knowing what you want is the first step toward getting it. If you have goals you'd like to pursue, try writing them in a Goals journal. In what you want to accomplish and then follow it aggressively. By writing what you want, you will surely realize that it's a helpful way to measure your progress, anticipate buffers, and stay inspired. At Ise Delhi, we have a sure way of knowing what we want, and then to achieve the same, how do we proceed?

Social and Mental Wellbeing

We weave into the fabric of our curriculum, activities that ensure the social and mental well-being of every Ise Delhi under our care. The famous Psychologist, Sigmund Freud rightly advocated the formative years as the most crucial years of any kind of learning. Children are like clay and every learning experience has an imprint on their minds. Therefore, Empowering Curriculum lays a lot of emphasis on experiential learning. Through various wellconceived and well-spread activities, we ensure young children acquire the social skills of adaptation, working in a team and understanding the importance of respecting and listening to others' views in a team. These basic skills go a long way in nurturing them towards a more acceptable adult in their later years.

Critical Thinking

We ensure and encourage critical thinking in Ise Delhiians. The concepts are put across in such a manner, where inputs are elicited from the young minds, discussion is always a hallmark of our classroom environment, which enables our children to think critically by wearing their 'Thinking Caps'. Sans values, no education is complete. Also, this encourages them to come up with their ideas and gives them the freedom to explore. We do not focus only on our children acquiring literary qualifications, but prepare them to be lifelong learners, which is the main and the real purpose of education.

Literacy for Life

Learning to respect their fellow travellers, companions, more so, the elders in and around their life share a major focus, in our curriculum while adopting the modern approach, we keep them grounded, and ensure that they are abreast with the traditional methods. The rich cultural heritage is our wealth. Children are familiarized with the same through contextual teaching & learning. We lay a lot of emphasis on building a relationship and fine-tuning our young minds in accordance. We at Ise Delhi understand the requirements of a future job market. It is estimated that in the next decade, the job market will go for a complete overhauling and simultaneously, the basket of skills required for being employable, too, will change. Future generations will need coding, data analysis, and entrepreneurial skills to name a few. Ise Delhi is a platform, where we'll strive to give them exposure, to all of this by collaborating with all the stakeholders from time to time.

Real World Applications

Besides these technical skills, we lay a lot of emphasis on the basic attributes like gratitude, decision making, emotional literacy, learning to apologize easily, ability to resolve conflict, empathy and compassion, developing mindful eating habits etc. all these objectives are weaved carefully in our curriculum so that children learn these automatically without much effort. Our faculty is not only highly qualified but also empowered and enriched from time to time to handle it with ease. Their continuous professional development is a major and essential component of this institution. We highly recommend they stay abreast with the latest pedagogies. This is achieved through several academic workshops, which they attend throughout the academic year. A lot of focus is laid on subject efficiency, we at Ise Delhi build perspective thinking minds, and we empower life on our campus, through our well-designed curriculum, which offers comprehensive standards, aligned social and emotional learning (SEL) in addition to our CPD programme. All of these are integrated into our academic programmes across classes. We follow pedagogies, which encourage students to think about "Real Issues". We empower them through several planned activities and programmes where they develop the skill of taking decisions and this skill of decision making goes a long way for the rest of their life, both on the professional as well as personal front. We at Ise Delhi follow a combination of both traditional methods of teaching and modern methods of teaching because the amalgamation of both forms of teaching will be beneficial for our Ise Delhi. In the Traditional Teaching Method, the teachers explain the concept to the learners with the use of a blackboard and chalk and ask the students to note down in their notebooks and memorize and recite the notes. On the other hand, Modern Methods of Teaching are a student-based and dedicated to more practical approaches.

Modern Teaching Methods

The teaching method focuses more on teaching the students for improving their intellect behaviour by using various new and innovative ideas rather than making them rote memorise the concepts to clear the examination. This new teaching method which we call the modern teaching method is more activity-based and centres around the learner's mind, keeping the learners as the primary target.

In this way, learners actively participate in the whole process to build their knowledge and sharpen their skills, this is also termed as "Constructive Approach". The need of the hour for students is to embrace the contemporary teaching methods which will also help in reducing the competition among the students, promote cooperation, and boost a healthy study environment. We focus more on modern teaching methods and include the following elements in our system:

LEARNER-CENTRED

The learning process involves learners and the focus is on learning that happens in the classroom. The facilitators are only there to guide the learners. The learners are motivated to bring their knowledge, past experiences and ideas which help them develop skills such as teamwork, problem-solving and decision making.

TASK-BASED OR ACTIVITY-BASED

Activity-based learning is the process of learning by doing. The activities play a key role in learning. The teacher organizes activities or tasks and engages students to learn through interactive activities like picture reading, storyboarding, puzzles and games. Moreover, Ise Delhi employs the use of smart technology such as interactive boards and VR headsets to make learning fun and engaging.

RESOURCE-BASED

Our teaching-learning process is based on the rich resources that the teacher organizes before introducing the topics in the classroom. Several important resource sites, such as YouTube channels, and helpful websites are conveyed to the learners and they are assigned a task to go home and get into the self-learning mode by visiting these resource sites and coming to the class prepared with the information and understanding of the topic. Then in the classroom, the teacher initiates a discussion, where all students are encouraged to participate by giving their inputs, interacting with peers and unanimously, the class arrives at certain generalizations which are noted by all in their notebooks. Based on this interactive discussion the facilitator raises certain high order thinking questions (HOTS), the parable answers to which are discussed in the class. The students are encouraged to ink this information in their own words.

INTERACTIVE IN NATURE

The focus here is always on the interactive mode of teachinglearning where the learner plays a pivotal role. The Empowering Curriculum is designed in such a way, that the faculty is aware that the major focus is on the learner.

INTEGRATIVE IN NATURE

The teaching methodology at Ise Delhi is integrative. We follow the "Arithmetic Approach" where the topics in each subject are interlinked for a better understanding of the learners. The interpretive approach ensures a "Pattern Practice" which leaves an indelible mark on the young learners.

PEER COLLABORATION

Through instructional activities, students learn to work cooperatively, and they appreciate their peers' work as well. In recent years, Ise Delhi School has introduced different modern teaching methods. The introduction of technology along with innovative ways to teach has brought a revolution to our school.

COLLABORATIVE LEARNING

In this modern teaching method, our teachers form a group of students where they can solve their problems, debate on different topics and clear their queries. This not only helps in developing social skills but also allows students to understand the subject faster. The kids learn to tolerate others, develop skills to listen to others and how to work in a team. They will be able to meet different personalities and can get a genuine review of their work.

GAMIFICATION

As the very word denotes the concept building especially in the elementary stage is put across to the young learners by introducing several games, experiments which could be in the form of puzzles, solving riddles etc. the class is divided into smaller groups and each group has a team leader. The instructions are given by the teachers in their classrooms and the rules are explained. All groups initiate, following the rules and are encouraged throughout, before they arrive at desired conclusions. The team leader discusses the outcome in the class and if required corrective measures are discussed mutually in the classroom itself. Through this, we encourage team-building skills and the skill of adaptation and adjustment where each learner learns to respect the views of fellow members of the group. Based on the conclusion of these activities, the teacher then introduces the concept with an objective, that each learner relates to it based on his or her experiential learning. Learning through gamification has shown tremendous positive outcomes, especially in the foundation years.

SPACED LEARNING

The attention span in young children is very less. So, it is here in this age group that, Spaced learning teaching method is used. It reduces the cognitive burnout of the children, and instead, it makes learning more effective and long-lasting. The gap is meant to refresh the mind of the learners and to make them prepare for the next part of the same lesson. This method gives the students intervals to inherit the knowledge and creates connections between learnings.

SELF-LEARNING

Curiosity pushes the learner to learn new and more and more always. Teaching students to operate the internet and find results themselves, helps them to be self-dependent and gives them a deep understanding of the content. Our teachers allow the students to bring in new ideas and work on them for the development of their brain and ability to work alone and to give them the feel of decision making.

CROSSOVER LEARNING

We at Ise Delhi, follow Crossover Learning which effectively engages learners and provides authentic and innovative results. We provide a formal setting i.e., a classroom to provide education and an informal environment of teaching i.e. field trips, visiting museums and seminars which generate curiosity and interest and increase understanding by asking questions to their teachers.

CASE-STUDY LEARNING

The projects refer to the task given to students entirely associated with the field they are studying. Here students are provided with a practical case study of their skill set and knowledge to provide an optimum solution to the problem. This method is followed in the elementary, middle and senior secondary levels of our school.

PROBLEM-BASED LEARNING

In this method, our teachers allocate the students a particular set of problems that will challenge their learning and the acquired skills so far. The students solve the problem using their intellect which enhances their skills and knowledge of the students to a greater extent. The learner is encouraged to think and arrive at a conclusion logistically

DESIGN LEARNING

This method promotes innovation and creativity in the students. The teachers offer a particular set of questions to the students that triggers the creative mindset and pushes them to arrive at successful innovative solutions to the issues.

THINKING-BASED LEARNING

The teachers offer the students a set of questions that challenges their memory making them focus more on an analytical approach to face the problem. This method focuses more on the theoretical approach to learning.

Conclusion

In this type of learning, a system of instruction, assessment, grading and academic reporting is followed that is based on students demonstrating that they have acquired the knowledge and skills that they are expected to learn as they progress through their education.

The inclusion of modern teaching methods in today's time is necessary to develop:

  • Decision-making skills
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Critical thinking ability

We truly believe in the diverse imaginations and observations, opinions and hopes of students hence, by empowering them, we engage them in meaningful learning. We give Ise Delhi, a voice through several forums, where their feedback is encouraged and respected. It surely enables us to better our best. We, at Ise Delhi School, constantly build classroom community through our daily dedications.

We strongly believe "Who dares to teach, must never cease to learn."
