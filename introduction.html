Introduction

When the student is ready, the teacher will appear. At Maxfort, we believe in excellence and inspire our children to achieve the highest with enthusiasm, excitement and enduring efforts. We prepare them to go beyond expectations by becoming persistent learners, independent, reliable, efficient decision makers with a progressive approach. Our broad curriculum ensures that students are not just well equipped for examinations but are ready for the world, work and life. School Life at Maxfort provides students an exclusive experience that is based on an all inclusive education. Offering an innovative and ambitious curriculum, we help students to achieve their highest potential. Maxfort is a meeting ground for students from diverse backgrounds which give a distinct environment for them to grow and develop. A nurturing, cohesive and culture of acceptance ensures that students can learn to evolve their personality and explore their own identity as they build relations, while attending school. Apart from academics, Maxfort is always teeming with miscellaneous activities that groom children, help to develop their personalities going beyond just relaxation or breaking routines. Enriching students in all dimensions, these activities provide a fulfilling experience that stays with the students for the rest of their lives.

Pre-Primary

The Pre-Primary years are the most crucial years in the life of a child since it is in this time that the child develops psychologically and studies indicate that nearly two-thirds of the child's intellectual abilities develop at this stage. The children develop their emotional and social temperaments as well as other core aspects of their personality during this time period only. We, at Maxfort, understand this. Hence, we provide a stimulating environment that is not only in accordance to the age and mental development of your child but is also thought provoking and suitably challenging to help him/her develop to his/her full potential. The environment at Maxfort is totally safe and secure with excitement and fun added to help children make the transition from home to school smoothly. The school life in the Pre-Primary stage is balanced with numerous activities that focus on physical, emotional, social, intellectual and spiritual development of the child. Our highly trained faculty guides the children through various activities encouraging them to play and learn in groups as well as individually to pace all round development. During this time, focus is laid on environmental, physical and technological education along with music and arts. Besides this, language, perception, critical thinking, communication, creativity and collaboration skills are also inculcated. Offering a strong academic and intellectual foundation, Maxfort Pre-Primary Life prepares your child fundamentally for the future.

Middlelevel

Middle school seeks to consolidate what the Junior school initiated. The school at this level endeavors to promote an able and diverse student body. It encourages in each member the traits of curiosity, creativity, leadership, flexibility, intellectual risk taking, independence of thought and respect for scholarship. Through a balanced mix of scholastic and co scholastic activities, it seeks to build a more egalitarian basis for social and personal growth. Children's freedom and space is respected to enable them to blossom into well groomed individuals. The school encourages them to develop a winning attitude that determines the quality of motivation and strength of commitment. The school instills social consciousness, concern for others and involvement in the community both within and without the school with a broad vision of humanity, compassion and sensitivity to issues around them. The school also focuses to foster the norms for social behavior and interaction. It also develops within students a will to face challenges in both work and play as also a sense of competence, self-confidence and emotional and physical well-being. Children are taught to appreciate the dignity of human labour. Sports and other co-curricular activities remain an integral part of the curriculum. A stress free, non-coercive and creative environment both within and outside the class enables the children to hone their skills and unfurl their innovativeness. The school also provides them with an opportunity for developing a strong bond of faith and mutual respect with their peers, teachers and parents. The focus is on making them good human beings who are morally upright and grounded. They are taught that "Success is nothing but getting up each time you fall". Minds are fine tuned to accept failure as a part of learning.

Health & Wellbeing

Maxfort is committed to the health and well being of all the students. Health has a tremendous impact on the achievements and growth of every child. We ensure adequate measures to protect and promote health of students. Health and wellbeing is incorporated into all aspects of school activities recognizing the fact that healthy students can learn and perform better hence reach their maximum potential. Encouraging healthy life style, linking education with health issues and providing effective, comprehensive and sustainable health to students is the formula on which we work. Sharing the responsibility of health and harmonizing the action required for well being of students, we initiate participation of parents and communities to coordinate meaningful efforts for health of students. Maxfort is well equipped with a medical room that facilitates and provides adequate health services to students within school hours. The medical facilities are monitored by a qualified staff including a Nurse who provides the necessary medical facilities to students when required.

Health services at Maxfort include:

Proper information to students and support in making well-informed decisions regarding their health and development. Health assessment for vision, hearing and other issues carried out regularly by doctors on panel and detailed records are maintained for each student. Health assessment for vision, hearing and other issues carried out regularly by doctors on panel and detailed records are maintained for each student. Health education sessions are conducted with the involvement of the teachers and students are mentored and made aware about various aspects of different diseases, first aid, healthy life style, diet and stress management etc. Regular health awareness campaigns are conducted for student's health and well being programs to improve awareness. Parental awareness regarding all critical issues encompassing student's health is on top agenda. Students with special requirements such as chronic medical problems or physical disability are taken care of in coordination with parents. Maxfort health care apparatus is well equipped and ready with medical expertise and medicines to provide immediate health care for students when required. In case of emergency, the school will provide first aid immediately. Simultaneously, parents and medical help will also be informed.
