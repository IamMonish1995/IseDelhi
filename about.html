The School

Everyone's journey to success is complimented with accomplishments. We have also achieved several milestones on the path of success. Since the beginning of this journey within a short span, we have been appreciated and rewarded notably.

Ours is an A league progressive school, where opinions of all stakeholders matter. We are a Co- educational day boarding school fully affiliated to CBSE and we have all three streams Science, Commerce and Humanities.

Our vision is to prepare our Ise Delhiians for life, by broadening their horizons, where they see a larger picture: We want them to realize their potential by providing them with an array of activities and educational pedagogies that enables them to unleash their creative streaks. We wish to give back to the society, responsible citizens who are upright and who make a difference in the society after leaving the portals of the school.

Our basic aim is to engage them in a healthy, learning environment. Where along with the mind, focus is laid on physical fitness. To raise Ise Delhiian who are compassionate towards the less fortunate.

Creating a 'Child Life' environment has always been a priority with us. The school premise is a second home to every Ise Delhiians in the true sense.

Collaborative teamwork is our forte. We demand respect, and instill the same in our young, budding Ise Delhiians. Our school is a 'No anger zone', because we know anger is just one letter short of danger.

Our Ise Delhiians are our pride and we are proud of them.
Together we CAN and we WILL make a difference that we seek.
Jai Hind.

Principle's Message

We want their learning to be joyful and harmonious in a happy surrounding, which is an essential ingredient for living a content life. Only then can they contribute to the society, which is the purpose of holistic education.

Ise Delhi is an institution that boldly predicts the future, by adapting itself to the ever changing global needs of education. We try to pursue new frontiers and embrace new educational techniques. We have taken it upon ourselves to ensure that each child is educated not only in the core subjects but also in the lessons of life like honesty, humility, compassion and sincerity. Our Ise Delhiians are the focal point of our entire educational programme. We follow the enquiry based integrated approach which forms the core concept of curriculum transaction. Our approach is child centric and we strive to raise the bar of excellence year after year, besides ensuring that they are employable after leaving the portals of the school. For this, we expose them to a wide variety of skills.

School Overview

Ise Delhi is a school where students can realize their dreams and aspirations, where they can relate their passions with career possibilities and where resources and opportunities to meet these decisions are well taken care of.

We are committed to the well-being and growth of every student at Ise Delhi. We cultivate and foster a culture of high objectives in both academics and moral values. Our education system encompasses a curriculum that helps to develop the knowledge, aptitude and personality of students inspiring them to become successful and conscientious citizens. Continuously leveraging all resources at various levels, we ensure that the education system proves out to be responsive, integrated, easily accessible and of high quality fostering holistic learning.

Engaging students, addressing their curiosity and developing creativity, commitment, courage and capability to solve problems is our single minded goal.

Providing the best amenities and tools to help students reach their highest potential, despite their individual limitations and circumstances helps them to become competent. We consistently work towards this aspiration.

At Ise Delhi, we aim to make students better equipped to adapt, realize and excel despite the, numerous challenges of life.
