The Campus

Maxfort School is located at Paschim Vihar, West Delhi, only few km away from the nearest Metro Station. The school campus is blessed with a clean, natural setting with the Paschim Vihar, District Park environs around it. This park is home to many birds and huge trees rejuvenating the atmosphere at Maxfort with fresh air.

Spanning an area of 2.5 acres, the school campus itself offers lush green lawns that create a tranquil environment, a must for the health and growth of young learners.

The school building is built in modern contemporary style with 54 Classrooms and 5 other rooms that are fully furnished with specially designed furniture. The school campus includes specific area marked for academic and administration staff, Reception Hall, Computer Room, Art Class, Library, and a Medical centre.

There are areas for indoor and outdoor sports, other energetic activities and a swimming pool. Adequate restrooms with modern facilities including sensory taps are provided for the convenience of the students.

Security of the students is one of the top priorities of the school. The school is well secured by a prominent security agency having efficient security guards who monitor and control all the exit and entry points to the school. All Maxfortians are required to carry their ID cards as they move around the school campus.

Spacial Rooms

Lbirary:
Maxfort has a well stocked spacious library with modern furniture, lot of great books and a cool ambience that facilitates reading and learning. Students can make use of the vast reading material and internet access for research and in-depth study. There is also a large collection of CDs on various topics.

The library at Maxfort promotes a culture of self-study and leisure reading by offering academic as well as fictional books. Library is a rich resource to offer instruction material and professional know-how to update the teachers. The vibrant premises are always brimming with students looking for learning assets and reading material for leisure and pleasure.

The Library is managed by efficient and friendly staff that assists the students in their exploration for the right books making suggestions and recommendations as per the students needs, interests, age and reading level.

Music Rooms:

The Maxfort Music Room is brimming with enthusiastic Music lovers. Equipped with all types of instruments, the students can learn both vocal and instrumental music.

Encouraged by highly skilled, talented and professional teachers, the students can explore the world of Music and enhance their potential accordingly.
Dance Rooms:

Maxfort Dance Room occupies expansive space providing students enough room to stretch their arms and legs in rhythm with aesthetic movements. Well trained mentors and masters guide and train the students about the various art forms, thereby preparing them to give a creative expression to their personality.

Medical Rooms:

The Medical Room at Maxfort has state-of-the-art facilities to handle all types of medical needs and emergencies. Trained Medical staff and assistance is there to help students in time of need.

Sports Rooms:

Seeking to promote a lifelong approach to fitness, Maxfort provides sports facilities in various arenas.
Outdoor facilities include Skating, Cricket, Basket Ball, Yoga, Lawn Tennis and Swimming. Indoor facilities are provided for Chess, Badminton, Soft Gym, Ball Pool, Hoopla, Snakes-Ladder and Carom Board.

Computer Rooms:

Maxfort School is equipped with computer facilities with advanced machines and newest software and applications. Aiming to make the students tech-savvy, we have highly professional staff that teaches numerous skills to the students. The students not only learn computers as a separate subject but are also taught to integrate these skills to learn other subjects.

Art And Craft Room:

The Art and Craft Room at Maxfort is buzzing with activity and the students are provided with an opportunity to express themselves in drawing, painting and Crafts. As the students learn to use colours, they are able to give expression to their joy, hopes and anxiety in various hues.

At Maxfort, we make available to the students all types of mediums that include oil pastels, water colours, pencil colours and more. The students are also taught craft work using beads, glass pieces, sequins and other innovative material.
